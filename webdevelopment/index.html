Introduction

This website contains my work experience in chronological order for viewers. The purpose of this website is to display most of my experience as a worker and self-reflect on the different types of jobs and settings I have worked in. This website aims to take the viewers on a journey of most of my past and current work experiences. This journey will include the lessons I have learned as a worker. As well as a personal reflection about each of the different job positions. This will also share the different strengths I've discovered I had while working at each job. This website intends to share a more detailed work experience that I cannot fully explain in my resume. After viewers have seen the content shared on this website, they will know my qualities as a worker. This website will also demonstrate my basic understanding of web development and design.
