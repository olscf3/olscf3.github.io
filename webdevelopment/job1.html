Jobs I have Worked

Taco Bell

In the summer of 2021 after my birthday, I decided to start working at Taco Bell for my first job. I began work as a Customer Service Champion. This job position's purpose was to take customer's food orders and handle their payment transactions at the drive-thru and lobby. The tasks of this job are to make sure the lobby and drive-thru are clean and stocked with food and drink materials. This consists of sauce packets, lids, straws, soda syrup, paper bags to put food in, and sweeping the floors. After mastering this job position, I began training as a Food Service Champion. The purpose of this position is to make the food for customers in the drive-thru and lobby at a fast pace. Tasks within this position consisted of making sure everything was swept, every ingredient in the kitchen was stocked, and wiping off materials when needed. I left this job in August of 2022 to work for Hy-Vee.
